Our General Experience

Madison's Opinion

  • Throughout the whole game, I got to see the absurdly wide range of interactions with these pick-up artists and the scary realities of hook-up culture.
  • In each encounter with every pick-up artist, the conversation would start normally with small talk but slowly progressed to make me feel weirded me out and even often time be confused what the tactic was even being used to seduce me as the player.

  • I had even played with my rooomate and there would be times both of us would have to pause the game to process and react to certain actions that happened.
  • The thing that unsettled me the most was that the game wasn’t far from reality and often times I found the uncomfortable conversations I would have in the game to be some of which I could see myself having to have in real life. The pick-up artists would often make sexist remarks, use manipulation tactics, and try taking hold of the conversation to get their way, similar to possible real encounters women have to have with similar types of men in real life and even my own encounters with people like that.

  • After learning about how the pick-up artsists are like in real life and learning about the techniques they tend to use, I got to appreciate the level of insanely accurate detail the game was made with.
  • The game overall truly opened my eyes to an entire community dedicated to techniques and actions as displayed in the game and the wide impact this community has had on others in the world.
